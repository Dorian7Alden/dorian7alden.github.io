### 📖 Accumulation



`statistically`    /stəˈtɪstɪkli/

(adv.) 统计上，根据统计数据来看:

- <u>Statistically</u>, you're more likely to get in a car accident close to home.

**Collocations**: `statistically significant`    `statistically speaking`    `statistically impossible`    `statistically prove`    `statistically analyze`

**Related**: `data`    `probability`    `correlation`    `demographics`    `statistic`    `quantitatively`    `empirically`

`based on`

基于，根据:

- The movie is <u>based on</u> a true story from the 1980s.

**Collocations**: `~ facts`    `~ evidence`    `~ a true story`    `~ research`    `~ experience`

**Related**: `on the basis of`    `according to`    `depending on`    `founded on`    `grounded in`    `derived from`    `in light of`

`specialty`    /ˈspeʃəlti/

(n.)(c) 专长，专业:

- His <u>specialty</u> is designing custom software for small businesses.

(n.)(c) 特产，特色菜:

- The wood-fired pizza is their <u>specialty</u>, so you should definitely try it.

**Collocations**: `medical specialty`    `area of specialty`    `specialty coffee`    `specialty shop`    `specialty field`

**Related**: `expertise`    `specialization`    `niche`    `forte`    `field`    `distinctive`    `signature dish`
