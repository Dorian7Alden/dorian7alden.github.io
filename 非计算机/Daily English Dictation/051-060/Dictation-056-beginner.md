## Daily English Dictation 56 - 2026/05/13 - beginner

### 🎧 Listening Practice



**My listening**: 

```
He sold his hair with cake around him. 
```

**Answer**: 

```
You sold us a hair with a cake around it！
```



**Pronunciation**:

You sol<u>d us a</u> hair wi<u>th a</u> ca<u>ke a</u>roun<u>d i</u>t！

> 这句表达不规范，不推荐这么用，rude。应该用 on it 的表达形式
