## Daily English Dictation 52 - 2026/05/06 - intermedia



### 🎧 Listening Practice



**My listening**: 

```
Here is people running and walking the dogs, and having brunches on the padio.
```

**Answer**: 

```
There's people running and walking their dogs and having brunch on the patios.
```

**Pronunciation**:

There's people running and walking ~~th~~eir dogs and having brunch o<u>n the</u> patios.

> running vs jogging: run for goal, place, competitive reason; jog for health
>
> walking eir => nare	having brunch => haven brunch
