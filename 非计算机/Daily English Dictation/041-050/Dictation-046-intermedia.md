## Daily English Dictation 46 - 2026/05/01 - intermedia



### 🎧 Listening Practice



**My listening**: 

```
Hey, is the Kang na?
```

**Answer**: 

```
Hey, is it cold out?
```



**Pronunciation**:

Hey, i<u>s i</u>~~t~~ col<u>d ou</u>t?

`muggy`    /ˈmʌɡi/
(adj.) 闷热潮湿的:
- It's so <u>muggy</u> outside that my clothes are sticking to me.

**Collocations**: `muggy weather`    `muggy day`    `muggy night`    `feel muggy`    `hot and muggy`

**Related**: `humid`    `sticky`    `clammy`    `sultry`    `oppressive`    `damp`    `moist`

`humid`    /ˈhjuːmɪd/
(adj.) 潮湿的:
- The air feels really <u>humid</u> after the rain.

**Collocations**: `humid weather`    `humid climate`    `humid air`    `hot and humid`    `extremely humid`

**Related**: `muggy`    `damp`    `moist`    `dry`    `humidity`    `dehumidifier`    `arid`

`slippery`    /ˈslɪpəri/

**Collocations**: `slippery road`    `slippery floor`    `slippery when wet`    `slippery slope`    `slippery surface`
