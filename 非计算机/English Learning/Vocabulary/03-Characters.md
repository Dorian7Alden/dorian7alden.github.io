### 紧张

- nervous 紧张    anxious 焦虑
- have butterflies in one’s stomach  心里七上八下、慌慌的（超地道）
	- I have butterflies in my stomach before exams.
- on edge 坐立不安、紧绷着
	- He’s been on edge all day.
- tense  神经紧绷的
	- You look so tense.  你看起来好紧张。

- stressed out  压力大到崩溃、极度紧张
	- I’m stressed out about the presentation.
- panicky 慌慌张张的、恐慌的
	- Don’t be panicky. 别慌
- I’m <u>a bundle of nerves</u> right now.
