## Daily English Dictation 63 - intermedia



### 🎧 Listening Practice



**My listening**: 

```
There are places that I remember. All my life, those some have changed, some all ever not for better, some have gone, some remaine. All these places have their moments with loves in friends as you can recall. Some are dead and some are living. In my life, I love them all.
```

**Answer**: 

```
There are places I remember. All my life, though some have changed. Some forever, not for better. Some have gone, and some remain. All these places had their moments. with lovers and friends I still can recall. Some are dead and some are living. In my life, I've loved them all.
```

### 📖 Accumulation



`succumb`    /səˈkʌm/

(v.)(vi.) 屈服，屈从 (过去式 succumbed，现在分词 succumbing):

- He finally <u>succumbed</u> to their demands.

**Collocations**: `succumb to pressure`    `succumb to temptation`    `succumb to despair`    `finally succumb`    `succumb to blackmail`

**Related**: `yield`    `give in`    `surrender`    `capitulate`    `cave in`    `submit`    `relent`

---

(v.)(vi.) 死于 (疾病等):

**Collocations**: `succumb to illness`    `succumb to cancer`    `succumb to wounds`    `succumb to complications`    `tragically succumb`

**Related**: `die from`    `perish`    `pass away`    `expire`    `fatal`    `mortality`    `lose one's life`
