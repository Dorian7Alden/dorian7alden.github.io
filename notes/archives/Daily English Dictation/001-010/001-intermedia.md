### words

Hello, everybody, welcome to Daily Dictation.

This is my brand new Youtube Channel and everyday I'm gonna have about a sentence for you to dictate.

The next day, I'll give you the answer, I'll teach you the pronunciation and the meaning of the sentence. OK?

So all you have to do is listen to the video or the audio and do your best to dictate the sentence.

> 注：为什么是 is listen to the video... 而不是 is listening to the video...
> 
> 原句 is (to) listen 省略了 to 的不定式，表将来要做的事，而 is listening 表示的是正在做的事，与原意不符
> 
> 结构公式：All you have to do is + 动词原形 (完全正确、地道) 记为固定搭配即可 All ... do is + 动词原形
> 
> 前面有 **do**，后面可以**省略 to**，直接用动词原形，普通主语前面**没有 do**，就**必须用 to do**。Your goal **is to listen**…

Now, your goal should _be to listen_ once and write it down.

Two times? No problem. Three times? That's good. Four times? You gotta practice more. Five times? You gotta practice a lot.

Six times, seven times, eight times...... You gotta keep coming to the channel.

OK? So, some sentences are gonna be easy, some sentences will be difficult.

I've got sentences from news, sentences from sitcoms and movies.

I've got sentences from everywhere.

So, good luck everybody. Keep listening, check your dictation and do the Daily Dictation.

My name is Coach Shane again and thanks a lot.

Check out my other channels for other cool ESL stuff.

OK, Daily Dictation Number 1. Yeah, this is a little bit tough. This is from AP News. Associated Press News.

The link is _==in==_ the bottom. You can listen to the whole story.

> 表示「在（页面 / 屏幕 / 文末的）底部」这个**位置**，固定搭配是：at the bottom
> 
> at the bottom (of the page/screen) 在（页面 / 屏幕）底部
> 
> in the bottom 一般只用于「在容器内部的底里」，比如：There’s some water **in the bottom** of the cup. 杯底有点水。

I've taken one sentence, you guys listen to the sentence and do your best for dictation. You can do it, go for it!

So, how did you do? You can listen again, keep listening, that's OK. Tomorrow, I'll be back with the answer.
