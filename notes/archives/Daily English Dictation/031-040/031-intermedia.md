#### 2. breed
- **释义**：（动植物）品种；培育
- **本句用法**：a new breed of ... 一种新的…品种
- **同义替换**：strain（菌株/品系）、variety（变种）
- **例句**：
  - This new breed of rice can survive in dry conditions.
  - Scientists have bred a disease‑resistant strain of wheat.

### 🔗 短语与固定表达

#### 1. love to hate
- **含义**：又爱又恨；乐于讨厌（带有调侃或矛盾情感的意味）
- **结构**：love to + 动词原形
- **例句**：
  - Jerry was the kind of guy you love to hate.  
    （杰瑞就是那种让你又爱又恨的家伙。）
  - I love to complain about traffic, but I still drive every day.

#### 2. protect against
- **含义**：预防；保护…免受…侵害
- **近义表达**：shield ... from ... / guard against
- **例句**：
  - Eating vegetables may protect against certain types of cancer.
  - This vaccine protects against the flu virus.
