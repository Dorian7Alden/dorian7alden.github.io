`go hand in hand`    [ (idiom) ]
密切相关，同时发生（常指两件事自然关联）:

- Books and chocolate just <u>go hand in hand</u> for a cozy afternoon.

**Collocations**: `~ with relaxation`    `~ with pleasure`    `~ in a perfect life`    `~ naturally`    `~ for many people`

**Related**: `be closely tied`    `complement each other`    `go together`    `pair well`    `be inseparable`    `be two sides of the same coin`    `link up`

`you know what`    [ (👻informal) ]
（口语）你知道吗，听我说（用于引出话题或强调）:

- <u>You know what</u>, we really do have the perfect life here.

**Collocations**: `and ~,`    `~, I think...`    `well, ~,`    `~ else?`    `~ I mean?`

**Related**: `guess what`    `by the way`    `come to think of it`    `as a matter of fact`    `incidentally`    `actually`    `look here`
