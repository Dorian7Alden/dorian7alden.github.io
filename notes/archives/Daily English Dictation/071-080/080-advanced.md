## Daily English Dictation 80 - advanced



### 🎧 Listening Practice



**My listening**: 

```
Olives are the gold under li see ya. Spanis world orangest biggest olive producer. And under li cer on 80 percent of the Spanish world.
```

**Answer**: 

```
Olives are the gold in Andalucia. Spain is the world's biggest olive oil producer, and Andalucia accounts for 80% of Spanish oil.
```
