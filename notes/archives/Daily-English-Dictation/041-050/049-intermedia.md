`stay mum about`    (👻informal)

对…保持沉默，绝口不提:

- The company is <u>staying mum about</u> the layoffs until the official announcement.

**Collocations**: `~ the details`    `~ the reason`    `~ the investigation`    `~ the incident`    `~ the allegations`

**Related**: `keep quiet about`    `remain silent on`    `hush up`    `not breathe a word`    `clamp down on info`    `play it close to the vest`    `button one’s lip`

`conspiracy`    /kənˈspɪrəsi/

(n.)(u) 阴谋活动，密谋策划:

- Several employees were arrested for <u>conspiracy</u> to commit fraud against the company.

(n.)(c) 阴谋，密谋计划:

- The press uncovered a <u>conspiracy</u> to hide the safety test failures.

**Collocations**: `criminal ~`    `~ theory`    `~ charge`    `uncover a ~`    `~ to murder`

**Related**: `plot`    `scheme`    `collusion`    `intrigue`    `cover-up`    `cabal`    `racketeering`
