## Daily English Dictation 51 - 2026/05/06 - expert



### 🎧 Listening Practice



**My listening**: 

```

```

**Answer**: 

```
And why do you wanna go there? I know it's had a bad press recently, but it's the place that's taken my fancy!
```

**Pronunciation**:

An~~d~~ why do you wanna go there? I know it'<u>s ~~h~~a</u><u>d a</u> ba~~d~~ press recently, but it's the place that's taken my fancy!

>
> S N L are strong sound. 7 Hs: he, her, him, his, had, has, have
