## Daily English Dictation 67 - advanced



### 🎧 Listening Practice



**My listening**: 

```
History is amazing. We took thousands of years to develop as great civilizations took flash to change. One hundred mannerd to courtest and five hundred fifteen means. Manned on the Mexico in fifty nineteen. It took only a couple of years to forever change the land. That is now the Mexico.
```

**Answer**: 

```
History is amazing. What took thousands of years to develop as great civilizations, took a flash to change. When Hernando Cortez and 550 men landed on the Gulf of Mexico in 1519, it took only a couple of years to forever change the land that is now Mexico.
```

**Pronunciation**:

History is amazing. What took thousands of years to develop as great civilizations, took a flash to change. When Hernando Cortez and 550 men landed on the Gulf of Mexico in 1519, it took only a couple of years to forever change the land that is now Mexico.
