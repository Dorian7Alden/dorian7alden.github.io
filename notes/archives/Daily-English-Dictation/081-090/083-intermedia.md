## Daily English Dictation 83 - intermedia



### 🎧 Listening Practice



**My listening**:

```
In a city full of a showy sides. These are more humble creations standing by until that stratching on March April when it is the time to blocks.
```

**Answer**:

```
In a city full of showy sights, these are more humble creations standing by until that stretch in March and April when it is time to bloosom.
```

**Pronunciation**:

> site: a specific place
>
> sight: a area with sites
